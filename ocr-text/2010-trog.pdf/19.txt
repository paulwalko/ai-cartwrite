Within the last year:
White Nose has spread even further. It now stretches from Ontario to Western Tennes-
see, and hundreds of caves are affected.
Local caves have continued to become infected and include:
Clover Hollow,
Newberry-Bane,
Tawneys,
dy Penley,
Starnes,
Coon,
Shires,
Newcastle Murderhole, Stonley(Tazewell).
Bud-
WNS signs have also been observed at:
Pighole,
Beer Can Chasm,
Repass Saltpetre
Higgenbotham.
Throughout this the VPI Cave Club has continued to cave while following decontamina-
tion and regionalization protocols.
For more information you can check out:
