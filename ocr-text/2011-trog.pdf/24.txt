HOW TO SET UP A PRUISK SYSTEM WITH
HELICAL KNOTS
ALEX BOOKER
Step 1. Cut two lengths of
7mm cord 9-10 feet long
and one length 5-6
feet long
Step 2. Take the long pieces of cord and tie a 24
inch loop in each and a 1 inch loop in the small
cord using a bowline or figure 8 knot. Make sure
to back off all knots.
Step 3. Take the 24 inch loops and wrap around your boots as pictured.
